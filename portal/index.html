How it works

From opportunity to introduction, quietly

STEP 01

Read the opportunity

Each opportunity describes a real, current opportunity, anonymised, but verified by us first-hand, usually across a table or on a call. Enough to know if it fits. Never enough to identify anyone.

STEP 02

Introduce yourself

Two minutes at the desk, who you are, your volumes, your systems, what you're after. Chris or Will reads every profile personally. Nobody else sees it.

STEP 03

A person weighs the fit

Not an algorithm's guess. One of us reviews every request, usually within two working days. If it isn't right, we'll tell you why, and keep looking on your behalf.

STEP 04

A personal introduction

When both sides agree, we introduce you directly, names, faces, facilities. From there the conversation is yours; we stay close to help it land.
