Privacy Policy

The NexPoint Network · Last updated 15 June 2026

This explains what we collect when you become a member of The NexPoint Network, why, and your rights over it. We keep it plain.

Who we are

NexPoint ("we", "us") is the data controller for the information you provide through The NexPoint Network. You can reach us at hello@nexpoint.co.uk.

What we collect

When you create a membership we collect:

  • Your name, email address, and (optionally) your mobile number;
  • Your company, and the profile details you give us in the membership questions, your type of operation, region, volumes, goals, and any pressing needs you choose to share;
  • A password, which is stored securely (hashed) by our authentication provider. We never see or store it in plain text.

Why we use it

We use your information to operate your membership, match you to relevant opportunities ("briefs"), and make qualified introductions on your behalf. We may email you when a new brief matches your profile, or to follow up where there is an obvious fit. Our lawful bases are performing the service you signed up for and our legitimate interest in running the Network; for optional marketing, your consent, which you can withdraw at any time.

Who we share it with

We use a small number of trusted providers to run the service: Supabase (secure account storage, hosted in the EU), Pipedrive (our customer relationship system), and Resend (email delivery). They process data only on our instructions.

Your identity is never shared with the party behind a brief until you explicitly agree to an introduction. We do not sell your data, and we do not share it for advertising.

How long we keep it

We hold your information while you are a member and for a reasonable period afterwards so we can pick up the conversation again. You can ask us to delete it at any time.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law you can ask us to give you a copy of your data, correct it, delete it, or restrict or object to how we use it. To exercise any of these, email hello@nexpoint.co.uk. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk).

Cookies and storage

We use your browser's local storage only to keep you signed in. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.
